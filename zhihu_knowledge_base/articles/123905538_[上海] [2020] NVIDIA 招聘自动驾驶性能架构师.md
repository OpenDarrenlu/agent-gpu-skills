鉴于现在的特殊情况，从面试到入职应该都是在家办公，有兴趣的同学可以发邮件到 mlv@nvidia.com

下面是 JD

WHAT YOU’LL BE DOING:

You will work on delivering most efficient software for GPU and PVA for Autonomous Driving solutions on NVIDIA DRIVE AGX Platform
You will analyze, prototype and optimize key applications for both existing and new architectures for GPU and PVA
You will build model to predict performance, power and reliability on future architectures and propose and evaluate new architecture features
You will be involved in crafting tools to analyze, simulate, validate and verify application performance and energy consumption
You will collaborate with different teams to improve the GPU and PVA architectures to extend the state of the art in performance, efficiency, reliability and programmability

WHAT WE NEED TO SEE:

Masters or PhD or equivalent experience in relevant discipline (CE, CS&E, CS, AI)
Excellent C/C++ programming and software design skills
Strong background in computer architecture, high performance computing
Performance modelling, profiling, debug, and code optimization or architectural knowledge of CPU and GPU
GPU programming experience (CUDA or OpenCL) desired

WAYS TO STAND OUT FROM THE CROWD:

GPU and DSP programming, performance analysis, modelling and optimization experience
Autonomous vehicle software development experience
Expertise in characterizing and modeling system-level performance, executing comparison studies, and documenting and publishing results
Experience in deep learning, computer vision and self-driving car domain
